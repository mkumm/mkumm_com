## Why Am I Writing A Blog?

I don't have advertisements so it's not for money. I don't even have analytics on this site so my ego isn't even enlarged by your visit. 

This blog represents a minor avenue of creation. I hope it benefits you by: helping you solve an Elixir problem; inspire tasting a new beer; maybe get some helpful expat information. The pinnacle of success comes If you consider a new view, get inspiration for a new idea, or bolster your current opinion through complete disagreement from what I write.

## Reboot Number...

Kinda like the Spiderman or Batman reboots, I lost track on how many there are. This one does feel different to me though. More personal, more vulnerable, and far fewer expectations.
