## Why Am I Writing A Blog?

I don't have advertisements so it's not for money. I don't even have analytics on this site so my ego isn't even boosted by your visit. 

This blog represents one avenue of creation. I hope it benefits you by: helping you solve an Elixir problem; inspire tasting a new beer; maybe get some helpful expat information. The pinnacle of success comes If you consider a new view, get inspiration for a new idea, or bolster your current opinion through complete disagreement from what I write.

## Reboot Number?

I don't know how many blogs I have maintained in the past, but this one feels very different to me. More personal, more vulnerable, fewer expectations.
