## Why Am I Writing A Blog?

It certainly isn't for money, as I don't have advertisements. I don't even have analytics on this site so my ego isn't even boosted by your visit. This blog is for personal reasons but, if you happen to benefit from an Elixir solution, hear about a new beer, or get some travel tips, I won't complain. If someone considers a new view, picks up an idea, or even bolsters their opinion through complete disagreement from what they read; I could ask for no greater result. 

Writing publicly requires more vulnerability than keeping a private journal, and it also requires more disciplined thinking. Both of which I could use more of. 



## Reboot

All in all, this might be my seventh attempt at writing and maintaining a blog. I am hoping that this time I have more to say. Feel free to say hello on [twitter](https://twitter.com/mkumm).
