## Profile Photo

Thanks to those who commented that they really liked my new profile photo, basically a cartoon rendering of me. Like a lot of us, I have really been enjoying all the AI stuff that suddenly became available, especially [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/) and [midjourney](https://midjourney.com/).

## Create a Cartoon Version of a Photo

1. Set up your accounts on [midjourney](https://midjourney.com/) and connect [Discord](https://discord.com/) (yeah, you have to use the bot in Discord to interact with [midjourney](https://midjourney.com/)).
2. Upload your image as a message.
3. Right click on the image you just uploaded, and select "copy link"
4. Create a prompt
```txt
"/imagine: <paste link> as a cartoon"
```
1. Wait for the bot to deliver 4 versions. Either [u]pscale the one you like or ask for 4 more!
